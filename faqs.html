Frequently Asked Questions

What is CineWatch?

CineWatch is your ultimate entertainment platform for streaming and discovering movies and TV shows. We also offer a comprehensive cinema guide to help you find and book tickets for the latest releases in theaters near you.

How do I stream movies and TV shows on CineWatch?

After creating an account (or logging in), you can browse our extensive library of movies and TV shows. Simply click on a title to view its details, and if available for streaming, you'll see an option to "Watch Now".

Is CineWatch free to use?

CineWatch offers a range of content, some of which may be free, while premium content or certain features might require a subscription or purchase. Please check the specific content details for more information.

How can I find cinema showtimes?

Navigate to the "Cinema Guide" section of our website. You can filter by city and browse available cinema halls and their current showtimes for various movies.

Can I book movie tickets through CineWatch?

Yes, our Cinema Guide integrates a multi-step booking modal that allows you to select showtimes and proceed with booking tickets for your preferred cinema halls. (Note: Actual booking may link to third-party providers.)

How does the dark mode feature work?

You can toggle between light and dark mode by clicking the moon/sun icon in the header. Your preference will be saved for future visits.

How do I add a movie/show to my Watchlist?

When viewing the details of a movie or TV show, you'll find an "Add to Watchlist" icon or button. Clicking this will save the title to your personal watchlist for easy access later.
