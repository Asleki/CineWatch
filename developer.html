About Me

Hello! I'm Alex Malunda, a passionate full-stack developer with a keen interest in creating intuitive and engaging web experiences. My journey into programming began with a fascination for how digital products are built, evolving into a dedication to crafting robust and user-friendly applications.

I specialize in front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a strong foundation in responsive design and modern web standards. On the back-end, I have experience with Node.js and various database solutions. CineWatch is a personal project born out of my love for cinema and television, aimed at providing a seamless platform for discovering and engaging with content.

My Journey in Web Development

My path to becoming a developer has been driven by continuous learning and hands-on experience. I believe in the power of code to solve real-world problems and enhance daily lives. Key milestones include:

  • Self-Taught Foundations: Began with online courses and tutorials, building a strong understanding of core web technologies.
  • Project-Based Learning: Continuously working on personal and collaborative projects to apply theoretical knowledge and explore new frameworks.
  • Focus on User Experience: Always striving to ensure that the applications I build are not just functional, but also delightful and easy to use.
  • Adaptability: Committed to staying updated with the latest industry trends and evolving technologies to deliver cutting-edge solutions.

My Projects

CineWatch

CineWatch is a comprehensive movie and TV show streaming platform design, focusing on intuitive user experience and dynamic content display. Currently, I am actively implementing download and uploading features, and also developing a system for users to write reviews for shows.

Key Features:
  • Dynamic Hero Slideshow
  • Categorized Show Listings with Carousels
  • Interactive Search Functionality
  • User Account Management (Login/Logout, Watchlist)
  • Dark Mode Toggle
  • Download and Upload Features (In Progress)
  • User Review System (In Progress)
